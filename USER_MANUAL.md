## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Go to the school portal website
2. Click **"Sign Up"** button
3. Fill in the form:
   - Your full name
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters, must include uppercase, lowercase, and number)
   - Select your role (Student, Parent, Teacher, or Admin)
4. Click **"Sign Up"**
5. You'll see a success message
6. You'll be redirected to the login page

### Logging In

1. Go to the portal login page
2. Enter your email and password
3. Select your role from the dropdown
4. Click **"Sign In"**
5. You'll be taken to your dashboard

### Password Requirements

## For Students

### Your Dashboard

When you log in, you'll see:
- **My Results** - Check your academic performance
- **Assignments** - View pending and completed assignments
- **Timetable** - See your class schedule
- **Library** - Access learning resources

### Checking Your Results

1. From your dashboard, click **"Check Results"**
2. Select your class from the dropdown
3. Click on your name
4. Enter your result password (get this from your teacher)
5. View your complete result sheet
6. Click **"Print Result"** to print or save as PDF

### Your Profile

1. Click **"My Profile"** in the sidebar
2. View your personal information
3. Change your password if needed
4. Update your details

### Error Messages?

**Common errors:**

**"Session expired"**
- Your login session has timed out
- Simply log in again

**"Unable to connect to server"**
- The server might be down for maintenance
- Check your internet connection
- Try again in a few minutes

**"Invalid credentials"**
- Your email or password is incorrect
- Check for typos
- Make sure Caps Lock is off
